The Copernicus’ Theory

The Copernicus’ Theory was the first Original Serie created exclusively by Noah Studio. Released in 2016, The series had only one episode and 65,000 views on YouTube.

Copernicus is a type of fish called mudskipper, commonly mistaken by evolutionists as a missing link. He lives in Charles Darwin’s library and is keepeth as a pet. Very smart and intuitive, Copernicus started to develop knowledge sufficient to refute the theory of evolution and bring back the wise knowledge about creationism.

Unfortunately, the original final video was lost in an accident, but the screenshots and the character-making process were saved.
