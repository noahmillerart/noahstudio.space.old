Fritzi Fragezeichen

For Hope Media Europe (Germany), Fritzi Fragezeichen (Fritzi Question Mark) is a character designed and animated exclusively by Noah Studio. A total of 7 episodes were released in the kids Program called “Fribos Dachboden”. Fritzi is a smart girl who tries to explain difficult topics to kids.
