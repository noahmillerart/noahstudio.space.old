Noah Studio is now taking its first steps, built upon more than 15 years of experience in mission and design.

Noah Miller (The pen name of Bruno Tribst) began in 2005 as Web Designer. In 2007, he further honed his skills as a motion designer for Novo Tempo, an Adventist TV channel in Brazil. However, in 2011, Noah made a pivotal decision to embark on a new chapter by relocating to Lebanon, to work for Hope Channel Middle East.

Noah pursued art studies in Cyprus before expanding his horizons in Germany for four years, immersing himself in the local culture and honing his skills. Seeking further growth, he ventured to Thailand, where he specialized in 3D art, gaining valuable experience for two years. Bruno then embarked on a year-long journey in Canada, working in a studio and exploring farming.

Currently he resides back in Brazil, where he operates a small studio. He is happily married and has two children.
